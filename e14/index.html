Open Adobe XD

Choose any size artboard to begin.

When artboard opens, go to right side of window to transform.

Click on W and then type 1000.

Click on H and type 1000. Your artboard should be 1000 x 1000.

On the leftside of window, click on the circle.

While holding the shift button, click towards the middle of the artboard and drag to make a circle, until the size is W 475 and H 475 under the transform section on the right of the window.Or you can make any size circle and tpe in 475,for W and H on the right hand of screen. Then type 263 for x and241 for Y positions under transform.

Click once on the box next to the fill button on the right of the screen. On the bottom of the screen, make sure hex is chosen and type in #EDE07E. Click on the check next to border to deselect border.

Next, hold down the option (for Mac) or alt (for windows) and click on the circle and drag it to the right about an inch. Int Transform box, type 333 for x and241 for Y.

Choose the arrow from the toolbar on the left of the screen and click on the new cirle once. Click on box next to fill once. Make sure hex is chosen on the botoom and type in #FFFFFF. Make sure the border box is unchecked.

With arrow tool, Click on first circle, then click on the image on the top right of the screen with a small rectangle and larger rectangle underneath and a line going vertically down the middle. Also known as alighn center horizontally if you hover over that image.
